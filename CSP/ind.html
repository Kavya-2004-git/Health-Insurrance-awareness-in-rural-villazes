Welcome

This project aims to create awareness about health insurance schemes available to rural communities. Our goal is to ensure that everyone can access affordable healthcare without financial burden.

Why Health Insurance?

Health insurance helps families avoid high medical costs and provides peace of mind during emergencies. Awareness is the first step towards better healthcare.

  • Prevents financial burden
  • Access to quality hospitals
  • Cashless treatment available
